Our Story

Our story started with just four students from Information Technology branch in engineering got same level to insanity and became friends after 3 years i.e in 4th year of engineering. Two of them were friends with others and other two were alone. After many unsuccessful friendship attempts they four became best friends. They are were extremely "bhukkad". But also got no money. One day while roaming near the streets they discover a south indian dishes stall near there hostel that was cheap compared to others. All of them got delighted and started going there everyday. As they were engineering students and as all of you know every enggineering student once thought of a startup and they were IT students which means "sone mein suhaga". They thought of this food network starting with a restaurent near there hostel and there came the name "Savioury Donuts"! They were saved from their hunger by these vada "English mein bolne se Donuts"! And here we are, with 70 outlets all over india and with millions of satisfied customers. we aim to feel your hunger and try to fulfill this with our food. Our network consists of people with small stalls as their only source of earning. We provide them with beautiful shops and hygenic techniques for cooking. We discover them with the help of you only. If you see a small stall owner who is in need of our help just invite us! And we will reach there as their Superman and yours too!
